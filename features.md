<h3>The College Administrator(college admin) is responsible for configuring all classes within the institution. This includes:</h3>
 

<h4>1️⃣ Creating and Managing Subjects </h4>– Define and organize subjects for student classes. 📚
<h4>2️⃣ Assigning Subjects to Faculty </h4>– Allocate subjects to the respective teaching staff. 🎓 <br>
<h4>3️⃣ Appointing Mentors for Classes </h4>– Designate mentors to guide and support students. <br>
<h4>4️⃣ Assigning Subjects to Individual Students</h4> – Customize subject allocations based on student requirements. 🎯<br>
<h4>5️⃣ Manage users -</h4> – Manage users and thier Access permissions.⚙<br>
